    Frequently Asked Questions

    Answers to Common Questions About Our Services

    Do you offer training programs for organizations?

    Yes, we offer customized training sessions for teams and individuals. Training can be conducted on-site or virtually, depending on your needs. You can also book your training directly through our website or by contacting us.

    What industries do you work with?

    EB Smart Solution Limited serves a variety of sectors including education, logistics, agriculture, retail, and public institutions. Our solutions are tailored to meet the unique needs of each industry.

    Can you develop custom software for our company?

    Absolutely. We specialize in building custom software and systems that match your specific business requirements. Whether it's a web application or internal system, we deliver scalable and secure solutions.

    Do you provide IT support after project delivery?

    Yes, we offer ongoing technical support and system maintenance to ensure your solutions continue running smoothly. You can also subscribe to one of our support packages for long-term assistance.

    How can we request a consultation?

    You can request a consultation by filling out the contact form on our website, calling our office, or sending an email. Our team will schedule a session to understand your needs and propose the right solution.
